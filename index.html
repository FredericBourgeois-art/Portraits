Concept

I create high-contrast, cinematic portrait sessions at iconic locations — Trocadéro, Bir-Hakeim, the grand Haussmann boulevards. My approach blends natural light with off-camera flash to sculpt faces with depth and intention.

Based in Paris, I work with international travellers, couples and individuals looking for a portrait that goes beyond the snapshot — something they'll keep forever.
